2. Understanding Your TandaPay Agreement

The Importance of Your Contract

Your TandaPay Merchant Contract is a legally binding agreement. It outlines the terms, conditions, and responsibilities for both TandaPay and your business. Reading and understanding it ensures a transparent and trusted partnership.

Key Sections in Your Agreement:

  • Services Provided: Details the TandaPay services you'll have access to.
  • Merchant Obligations: Your responsibilities, including fees and compliance with KYC (Know Your Customer) laws.
  • Fees: Clearly outlines any applicable transaction fees.
  • Conditions for Termination: Explains the circumstances under which the agreement can be ended.
  • Data Privacy & Security: Explains how we handle and protect your data and your customers' data.

Signing Your Contract:

You will typically need to sign the TandaPay Merchant Contract on:

  • Page 1: Effective date and party details.
  • Page 8 (or similar page with signature blocks): Your signature, name, designation, and date.
  • Page 10 (or final signature page): Often a final confirmation or witness signature area.
(Please refer to the specific contract provided to you for exact page numbers and signature locations.) You should have received a digital copy of your signed contract via email. If you cannot locate it, please contact us.

Our Commitment: Regulated & Secure

TandaPay is a legal entity, licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya (CBK). This means we operate under strict guidelines to ensure the safety and security of your transactions and data. 3. Getting Started: Setting Up Your TandaPay Merchant Account

Let's Get You Connected!

Follow these simple steps to become a TandaPay Merchant and prepare for your first transaction. Have your business information ready.

  1. Step 1: Sign-Up

    You have two primary ways to begin your registration:
    • Online: Visit org.tanda.africa to start the registration process. This is recommended for full merchant sign-up.
    • USSD (Alternative for some services): Dial *712# from your phone for certain functionalities.
    • Verify your email address via the confirmation link sent to your inbox if applicable.

  2. Step 2: Provide Your Business Information

    During registration or in your "Business Profile" / "Account Settings" section:
    • Enter your legal business name, address, and contact information accurately.

  3. Step 3: KYC (Know Your Customer) Documentation

    To comply with regulations and ensure a secure platform, we require some documentation. Please have clear copies ready to submit:
    • Business Permit: A valid permit for your existing business.
    • National ID: A copy of your National ID.
    • Passport Photo or Selfie: A clear, recent photo.
    • (If a registered company): You might also need your Business Registration Certificate/Certificate of Incorporation, KRA PIN certificate, and proof of address.

  4. Step 4: Account Verification & Till Number

    • Verification: TandaPay will verify the legitimacy of your business and documents. This helps maintain a secure ecosystem for all users.
    • Till Number: Once approved, you will receive your TandaPay Till number. Getting a TandaPay Till is free and instant upon successful onboarding.
    • You can then download the TandaPay App (or Tanda Trader App) from the Play Store to manage your account.
    • Set up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for enhanced security if available. We highly recommend this!

  5. Step 5: (Optional) Add Team Members

    If you have staff who need access, you can often add them as users with specific roles and permissions under "Team Management" or "User Settings" in the TandaPay portal/app.

Need help? If you encounter any issues during setup, please refer to our FAQ at [Link to TandaPay FAQ Placeholder] or contact support (details in Section 9).

4. Navigating TandaPay: Platform & App Walkthrough

Your TandaPay Command Center

Familiarize yourself with the TandaPay merchant portal (web, if applicable) and mobile app. These are your tools for managing transactions, viewing reports, and configuring settings.

Accessing TandaPay (USSD & App)

  • TandaPay App (Recommended for full features): Download from the Google Play Store after your account is approved. This app provides access to transaction management, reporting, payment tools, and customization.
  • USSD Code *712#: For quick access to certain payment functions like initiating payments.
    • To accept payment via USSD for a customer: The customer dials *712#, selects 'Pay', enters your TandaPay Till Number, and the amount.
    • To make a payment via USSD (as a merchant): You can dial *712#, select 'Pay', and follow prompts.

Core Features for Your Business:

  • Accepting Payments from Customers:
    • Customers can pay you from M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and Tkash.
    • STK Push: From the TandaPay App, you can enter the client's number and amount. This will send a prompt to the client's phone to enter their PIN and authorize the payment.
  • Withdrawing Your Funds:
    • Instantly withdraw your collected money to your preferred bank account or mobile money wallet (M-Pesa, etc.).
  • Paying Your Suppliers:
    • Use TandaPay to pay your suppliers directly to their Till numbers or Paybill numbers.
  • Managing Your Account (via App/Web Portal):
    • Unified Merchant Account: See all your transactions in one place.
    • Single Statement: Simplified financial reconciliation.
    • Real-time Dashboard: Track payments and gain insights into your financial operations. View recent activity and key metrics.
    • View detailed transaction lists and individual transaction details.
    • Manage refunds or process adjustments (as per TandaPay policy).
    • Configure account settings, business profile, and notification preferences.

Understanding Our Low Fees:

  • Collections Ksh 200 and below: FREE
  • Collections above Ksh 200: 0.4%
  • Pay Suppliers or Withdraw any amount: Ksh 10

Action: Log in to your TandaPay App now and spend 15-20 minutes exploring these sections. If a web portal is available, explore that too.

5. Your Go-To-Market Strategy: Focusing on High-Value Zones

Maximizing Revenue with TandaPay

To maximize your revenue with TandaPay, we encourage focusing your efforts on areas with high business potential.

Why Focus on High-Traffic Areas?

  • Increased Revenue: Malls, busy business districts, and affluent neighborhoods typically have a higher concentration of customers ready to make digital payments.
  • Improved Merchant Performance: Businesses in these areas often have higher transaction volumes.
  • Tech-Savvy Customers: Consumers in these locations are generally more inclined to adopt digital payment solutions for convenience and security.

Identifying Your Target Customers:

TandaPay is ideal for various retailers, including:

  • Retail Dukas, Mini Marts, and Supermarkets
  • Chemists and Pharmacies
  • Barbershops and Salons
  • Cosmetics shops and Boutiques
  • Electronics shops
  • Hotels and Eateries

Consider the daily trade volume:

  • Tier 1: Serving over 250 customers daily (e.g., minimarts, wholesale shops in busy areas).
  • Tier 2: Serving 100-250 customers daily (e.g., shops in apartment blocks, shopping centers).
  • Tier 3: Serving less than 100 customers daily (e.g., small independent structures).

Focusing on Tier 1 and Tier 2 in high-value locations can significantly boost your TandaPay transactions.

Key Strategies for Launch:

  1. In-Store Promotion & Signage:
    • Display "TandaPay accepted here" signage prominently (window decals, counter signs, QR code displays).
    • We may provide a starter kit with promotional materials. Check with your Account Manager.
    • Train your staff to mention TandaPay as a payment option.
  2. Staff Training:
    • Ensure your staff understands how TandaPay works and can explain its benefits to customers.
    • Conduct a quick training session on processing TandaPay transactions.
  3. Launch Offer / Incentive (Optional):
    • Consider a small discount or special offer for the first [X] customers who pay with TandaPay.
    • This can create initial buzz and encourage trial.

Tip: Focus on making the TandaPay option clearly visible and easy for customers to choose.

8. Tracking Your Success: Performance & Reporting

Measure, Analyze, Optimize

TandaPay provides tools to help you monitor your business performance, identify trends, and make informed business decisions.

Using Your TandaPay Dashboard:

  1. Access your merchant dashboard through the TandaPay App or web portal at [TandaPay Login URL Placeholder].
  2. Navigate to the "Reports," "Analytics," or "Statements" section.
  3. Here you can view real-time transaction data, generate reports, and get insights into your payment collections.
  4. Select the type of report you wish to view and choose the desired date range.

Key Metrics to Monitor:

  • Transaction Volume: The number and value of TandaPay transactions.
  • Customer Adoption: How many of your customers are using TandaPay.

  • Payout Reports: Track funds deposited to your account.

Using Data for Improvement:

  • Identify peak times for TandaPay usage.
  • Track the effectiveness of any TandaPay-specific promotions.
  • Understand customer behavior and preferences.
  • Use payout reports for financial reconciliation.

Regularly reviewing your TandaPay performance data is key to optimizing its benefits. This information helps you understand customer behavior and optimize your operations. Your Account Manager can also help you interpret these reports.
